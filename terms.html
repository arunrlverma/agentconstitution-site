2. What Agent Constitution Is

Agent Constitution is a tool that lets you define behavioral rules (guardrails) for your AI agents. The App stores your rules locally and lets you approve or deny agent actions via push notifications. The relay server is a stateless message queue that passes constitution check requests between the App and your agent.

  • Approving an action means the agent will proceed — ensure you understand what you're approving.
